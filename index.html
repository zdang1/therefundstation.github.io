FAQ

How does it work?

Simple! We come to you in our fully equipped truck and clean your bins right at your property. We use high-pressure washing and eco-friendly detergents to blast away grime, germs, and odours.

Do I need to be home?

Nope! As long as your bins are out and accessible, we’ll take care of the rest.

When do you clean the bins?

We clean on the same day as your regular bin collection, so your empty bin gets cleaned before it’s brought back in—no schedule changes needed!

What types of bins do you clean?

We clean it all—from regular household wheelie bins to large industrial dumpsters. No job too big or small!

What do you use to clean the bins?

We use an industrial-strength pressure washer and biodegradable, eco-friendly detergents that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment.

How often should I get my bins cleaned?

It depends on how you use them! Most customers choose a monthly or weekly plan to keep odours and germs under control.

Do you offer discounts?

Yep! New customers get 2 bins cleaned for the price of 1 on their first subscription. Plus, refer a friend and get an extra bin cleaned free on your next service!
