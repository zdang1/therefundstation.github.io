How You Can Make A Difference

Every container you return isn't just about earning a 10c refund — it's about making a real impact.

By recycling your bottles, cans, and cartons, you're helping reduce landfill, protect wildlife, save natural resources, and support vital local programs like schools, charities, and community events.

Together, one container at a time, we're building a cleaner, greener future for Central Queensland — and it all starts with you.
Start small. Make a big difference.

Drop off eligible bottles, cans, and cartons at your nearest Refund Station — it's quick and easy. Our friendly team will help you sort and count your containers, ensuring you get the maximum refund for your efforts.

💡 Tip: Clean containers process faster and help maintain hygiene standards!

Schedule a convenient pickup service for large quantities of containers. Perfect for businesses, schools, or events with substantial container volumes that need professional collection.

🚚 Service: Free pickup for 1000+ containers with advance booking!

Encourage friends, family, and colleagues to recycle their containers and join the movement. Share your environmental impact on social media and inspire others to make a difference.

🌱 Goal: Every person you inspire can prevent 100kg+ of CO₂ emissions annually!
